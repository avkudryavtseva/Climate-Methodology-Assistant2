8.2 Project Emissions 
Project emissions include start-up and emergency consumption of gasoline in the project activity, 
for which the sum shall not exceed 5% of the total fuel consumption under the project activity per 
each fleet per year. The project emissions from ethanol consumption that is renewable fuel are 
considered zero.
