8.2 
Project Emissions 
Project emissions must be determined following the procedures provided in the latest version of 
AMS-III.AJ.  
8.3 
Leakage Emissions 
Leakage emissions must be determined following the procedures provided in the latest version 
of AMS-III.AJ.
