4 APPLICABILITY CONDITIONS 
All applicability conditions in the most recent version of AMS-III.AV. must be met in addition to 
the following condition: 
• 
The risk of double counting of the reduced emissions from non-renewable biomass is 
assessed on a national basis by evaluating at validation and crediting period renewal 
whether there are REDD+ projects or jurisdictional REDD+ programs whose project 
boundaries overlap with the expected fuel source area (in the business-as-usual 
scenario). The project proponent must report on the findings of this assessment in the 
project description of projects applying VMR0015.
