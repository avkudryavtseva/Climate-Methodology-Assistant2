ADDITIONALITY 
Project proponents applying this methodology must determine additionality using the procedure 
described below: 
Step 1: Regulatory Surplus 
Project proponents must demonstrate regulatory surplus in accordance with the rules and 
requirements regarding regulatory surplus set out in the latest version of the VCS Standard.  
Step 2: Positive List 
The applicability conditions of the latest version of VCS module VMD0049 Activity Method for 
Determining Additionality of Electric Vehicle Charging Systems represent the positive list. The 
positive list was established using the activity penetration option (Option A in the VCS Standard). 
Projects that meet all applicability conditions of this methodology and VCS module VMD0049 
Activity Method for Determining Additionality of Electric Vehicle Charging Systems are deemed 
additional.  
Step 3: Project Method
