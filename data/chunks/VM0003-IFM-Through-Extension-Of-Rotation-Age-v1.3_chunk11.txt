Initial version released. 
v1.1 
20 Nov 2012 
Revision of Section 8.5.1.3. to account for the decay of carbon from the 
harvested wood products pool and to make explicit that the decay of the 
dead wood pool must not be assumed to be immediate.  
Other minor updates have also been incorporated into the methodology. 
Specifically, the applicability conditions were revised to clarify the types of 
forest management techniques which are eligible activities under this 
methodology. A modification was made concerning the timing by which FSC 
certification must be in place to allow projects to achieve certification by the 
start of the project crediting period.

29 Aug 2013

scenario.

Modified Section 4 to expand access to non-certified lands that are to 
become FSC certified.  
Modified Section 8.5.1 to improve accuracy of wood products accounting in 
equations 33, 35, 36, and 37.  
Updated Section 8.7.2, equation 47 to conform with the most recent VCS 
requirements for quantifying uncertainty.
