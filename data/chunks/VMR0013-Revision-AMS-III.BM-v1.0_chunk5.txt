4 APPLICABILITY CONDITIONS 
All applicability conditions of the most recent version of AMS-III.BM. must be met. 
The scope of AMS-III.BM. is expanded by including other two- or three-wheeled e-vehicles in 
type 7 projects and an additional category of type 9 projects, which covers e-bikes and other 
two- or three-wheeled e-vehicles in business-oriented activities.
