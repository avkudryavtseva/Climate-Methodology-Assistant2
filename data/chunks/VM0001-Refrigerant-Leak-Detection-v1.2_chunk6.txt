5 PROJECT BOUNDARY 
The physical project boundary is the set of retail stores in which automatic, real-time leak 
detection systems have been installed and HFC refrigerants are used. Where HVAC systems in 
these stores are supported by the same leak detection systems, the physical boundary includes 
these HVAC systems. 
The methodology covers the following categories of HFC emissions reductions from equipment 
within the project boundary: 
1) HFC emissions during DX refrigeration equipment operations and/or 
repairs/maintenance; and 
2) HFC emissions during seasonal top-offs and draw-downs.
