7 ADDITIONALITY 
Paragraph 12 of AMS-III.AV must be replaced by the following:  
12) The additionality of a project activity must be demonstrated by applying the most recent 
version of one of the following tools appropriate to the project scale:2 
a) VT0008 Additionality Assessment (for any projects) 
b) CDM TOOL21 Demonstration of Additionality of Small-scale Project Activities 
(for projects with annual GHG emission reductions of up to 60 kt CO2e in any 
year of the crediting period) 
c) CDM TOOL19 Demonstration of Additionality of Microscale Project Activities (for 
projects with annual GHG emission reductions of up to 20 kt CO2e in any year of 
the crediting period) 
 
 
2 Project proponents may demonstrate additionality by applying a tool indicated for use at a larger scale than the project 
activity, but not one indicated for use at a smaller scale than the project activity. For example, a project with 50 kt CO2e 
annual reductions may apply TOOL21 or VT0008, but not TOOL19.
