5 PROJECT BOUNDARY 
The spatial extent of the project boundary (Figure 1) encompasses the geographic area where: 
1) Initial waste biomass is sourced; 
2) Waste biomass is treated through eligible thermochemical technologies for the purpose 
of biochar production; and 
3) The final application of biochar in soils or non-soils occurs.
