The CDM methodology AM0125 applies to the production of geopolymer cement to displace 
Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC) that would have otherwise been produced and used in the host 
country. 
This methodology revision (VMR0012) includes a discount factor to account for emission 
reductions from upstream displacement of production of Ordinary Portland Cement, in alignment 
with the VCS Methodology Requirements. 
VMR0012 must be used with the latest version of AM0125. The procedures and requirements of 
AM0125 must be applied unless indicated otherwise.
