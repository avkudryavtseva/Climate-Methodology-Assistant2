This methodology applies to project activities that involve the destruction of methane emissions 
and displacement of a more GHG-intensive service by capturing landfill gas and flaring the 
methane, using it to produce electricity or thermal energy, and/or supplying it to consumers 
through natural gas distribution networks, dedicated pipelines, or trucks. It is globally 
applicable. 
VMR0016 must be used with the most recent version of ACM0001 Flaring or Use of Landfill 
Gas. The procedures and requirements of ACM0001 must be applied unless indicated 
otherwise.
