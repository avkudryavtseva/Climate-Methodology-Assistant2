This methodology quantifies the greenhouse gas (GHG) emission reductions from project 
activities that recover and/or collect ozone-depleting substances (ODS) or hydrofluorocarbons 
(HFC) from products and subsequently destroy them, including refrigerants, blowing agents, fire 
suppression agents, and propellants. Project activities that prevent the partial or total 
atmospheric release of ODS or HFC that would have occurred in the baseline scenario are 
eligible.  
This methodology is applicable in all countries that are a Party to the Montreal Protocol, 
including both Article 5 and non-Article 5 countries.
