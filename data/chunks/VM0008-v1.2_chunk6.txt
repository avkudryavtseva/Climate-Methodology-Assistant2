Leakage 
Appliances, heating/cooling equipment and/or mobile homes that are replaced must be 
properly disposed of and their disposal must be documented. The disposal documentation 
must confirm that:  
1) the appliances have been disposed of in a manner that prevents operation of the 
appliance; and  
2) the disposal procedure complies with applicable law and regulations. If not 
documented, CO2 emissions from continued operation of replaced appliances, 
heating/cooling equipment and/or mobile homes and HFC emissions from refrigerators 
or air conditioners must be accounted for as leakage.
