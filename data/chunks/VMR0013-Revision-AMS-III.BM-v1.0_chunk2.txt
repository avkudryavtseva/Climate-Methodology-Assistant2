The CDM methodology AMS-III.BM.: Lightweight two- and three-wheeled personal 
transportation applies to project activities that shift urban passenger transport to mechanical 
bicycles, tricycles, e-bikes, or e-tricycles by developing supporting infrastructure, such as bike 
lanes, shared bike programs (dockless or station-based), and bicycle parking areas. 
This methodology revision expands the scope of AMS-III.BM. to include other two- or three-
wheeled e-vehicles such as e-motorcycles and e-scooters. The revision also includes business-
oriented delivery services involving goods, food, or other tangible items. 
This methodology is globally applicable.
