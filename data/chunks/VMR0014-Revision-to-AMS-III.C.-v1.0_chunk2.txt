CDM methodology AMS-III.C.: Emission Reductions by Electric and Hybrid Vehicles quantifies 
greenhouse gas (GHG) emission reductions from project activities that introduce new electric 
and/or hybrid vehicles to displace the use of fossil fuel vehicles in passenger and freight 
transportation.  
This revision expands the applicability of the methodology to include electric mobile machinery 
(EMM), such as building and construction machines. EMM includes both battery-electric mobile 
machinery and plug-in hybrid electric mobile machinery. 
The methodology is globally applicable.
